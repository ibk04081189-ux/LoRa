About the Protocols

LPWA (Low Power Wide Area) networks like LoRa use different contention mechanisms to share limited spectrum efficiently.

Pure ALOHA

Immediate transmission upon packet arrival. Simple but prone to collisions as traffic increases.

Slotted ALOHA

Transmissions are synchronized to time slots. Reduces collision window by 50% compared to Pure ALOHA.

LBT (CSMA/CA)

Listen Before Talk. Checks if the channel is busy before transmitting, significantly reducing collisions in dense networks.

Theoretical vs Simulation

The gap between theoretical curves and simulation results often comes from the Capture Effect, where the strongest signal can still be received despite interference.
